# RAG AI Chatbot

## Overview

The RAG AI Chatbot is an intelligent conversational assistant that leverages Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) techniques to provide accurate and contextually relevant answers by combining the power of large language models with a knowledge retrieval component. This architecture enables the chatbot to ground its responses in up-to-date or domain-specific information, making it suitable for a variety of applications such as customer support, knowledge base querying, and more.

## Features

- **Retrieval-Augmented Generation**: Combines search and generation for accurate, context-aware responses.
- **Natural Language Understanding**: Handles complex queries in conversational language.
- **Flexible Knowledge Sources**: Connects to documents, databases, or web sources for dynamic retrieval.
- **Scalable and Modular**: Easily integrates with different platforms and data backends.

## Usage

1. **Start the chatbot service.**
2. **Ask your question or provide a prompt.**
3. **The chatbot retrieves relevant information and generates a grounded response.**

## Example

```
User: What is RAG in AI chatbots?
Bot: RAG stands for Retrieval-Augmented Generation, a technique that combines information retrieval with generative models to provide more accurate and up-to-date responses in chatbot applications.
```
